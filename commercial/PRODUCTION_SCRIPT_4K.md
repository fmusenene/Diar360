---

## Recommended Style

- **Color grading:** Warm, cinematic; dark tones with gold (#d4af37) accents
- **Typography:** Serif for headlines (e.g. Cormorant Garamond), clean sans for body
- **Music:** Inspirational, corporate, subtle strings/piano
- **Pacing:** Slow, confident; 6–10 seconds per section

---

## Assets Needed for 4K Production

- High-res project photos (4K or higher)
- CEO portrait (high resolution)
- Construction footage (optional B-roll)
- Logo (vector/SVG)
- Certification badges (high-res)
